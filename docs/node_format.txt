- Bnode format: 
+------+-------+------------+------------+------------+
| type | nkeys | pointers   | offsets    | key-values |
+------+-------+------------+------------+------------+
|  2B  |  2B   | nkeys * 8B | nkeys * 2B |    ...     |
+------+-------+------------+------------+------------+

- KV part format: 
+------+-------+-------+---------+
| klen | vlen  |  key  |  value  |
+------+-------+-------+---------+
|  2B  |  2B   |  ...  |   ...   |
+------+-------+-------+---------+
